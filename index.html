How CourseSync works

One place for your current classes, activities, future picks, and profile—built for students at Emerald High School and Fallon Middle School.

  1. 1

    Build your real schedule

    Add courses with teachers, rooms, ratings, comments, reactions, and activities. Bell times and course lists stay tied to your community.

  2. 2

    Map what is next

    Build primary and backup course lists with grade-aware AP, Honors, CP, and elective options.

  3. 3

    Compare with your people

Benefits

Know What Is Popular
